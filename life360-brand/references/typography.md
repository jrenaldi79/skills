# Life360 Typography

## Font Family
**Primary Font Family:** "Sofia Pro", sans-serif
**Fallback Fonts:** Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif

*Note: Custom web fonts like Sofia Pro may not render in all email clients. Always specify a web-safe fallback stack.*

## Font Stack Example
font-family: 'Sofia Pro', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;

## Usage Guidelines
- **Headings:** Bold (700), typically Black (#000000) or Dark Gray (#333333)
- **Body Text:** Regular (400), typically Dark Gray (#333333) or Downriver (#092C45)
- **Line Height:** 1.5 for body text
