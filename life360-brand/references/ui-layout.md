# Life360 UI & Layout Principles

## The "Card" Aesthetic
Our visual language mirrors the app experience: clean, contained content sitting on a neutral background.

### Card Specs
-   **Background:** White (#FFFFFF)
-   **Border Radius:** 16px (Standard for containers/cards)
-   **Shadow:** Soft, diffuse shadows (e.g., "0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)") to create depth and separation from the background.
-   **Padding:** Generous internal padding (30-40px) to let content breathe.

## Backgrounds
-   **Canvas Color:** Light Gray (#F6F5F8) is the standard canvas color. It provides accurate contrast for the white cards.
-   **Avoid:** Pure white backgrounds for the entire page (flattens the design) or heavy Purple backgrounds (overwhelming).

## Buttons & Actions
-   **Primary Action:** Life360 Purple (#8652FF) pill-shaped buttons.
-   **Radius:** 25px (Fully rounded/Pill).
-   **Text:** White, Bold (700).
