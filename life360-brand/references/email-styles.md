## General Principles

### Layout
-   **Visual Metaphor:** Content should sit within a white "Card" container floating on a Light Gray (#F6F5F8) background.
-   **Width:** Maximum 600px (Mobile optimized).

### Header & Branding
-   **Logo:** The Horizontal Wordmark is the primary asset. It must be transparent.
-   **Imagery:** Use authentic lifestyle photography (Human Connection) as the header background. Avoid plain gradients or seasonal holidays for general templates.

### Typography
-   **Headings:** Bold/Heavy weights (700-800).
-   **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Arial/Helvetica fallback stack).
-   **Color:** Dark Gray (#333333) on White (#FFFFFF) for maximum legibility.

### Buttons
-   **Style:** Pill-shaped (fully rounded).
-   **Color:** Life360 Purple (#8652FF) with White text.
